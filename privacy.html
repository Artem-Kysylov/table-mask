Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy is our priority. TableMask is operated by Spendly (trading as Syntax Labs) and is built with a strict "local-first" philosophy.

Who We Are

TableMask is operated by Spendly (trading as Syntax Labs). Spendly is the data controller responsible for your personal information when you use this service. In this Privacy Policy, "we", "us", and "our" refer to Spendly (Syntax Labs).

Local Data Processing

When you use TableMask to anonymize your text, CSV, or JSON data, all processing happens locally in your web browser via Web Workers. Your raw input data and files are NEVER uploaded to our servers, and we have absolutely no access to the content you process. Token maps used for the Reverse Mapping feature are stored strictly within your browser's temporary operational memory (RAM) and are completely wiped when you close the browser tab.

What Data We Collect

Spendly (Syntax Labs) only collects the bare minimum necessary to provide the service:

  • Account Information: If you purchase a Lifetime license, we store your email address to manage your account status and provide cross-device sync.
  • Payment Data: All payments are processed securely by our Merchant of Record, Paddle.com. We do not store or process your credit card information. Paddle manages this data in accordance with their own strict privacy policies.
  • Usage Data: We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand basic usage (like page views) without tracking individual users or intercepting user inputs.

Contact

If you have any questions about your data or how Spendly (Syntax Labs) processes it, contact us at hello@syntaxlabsapp.com.
