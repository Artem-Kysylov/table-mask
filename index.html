Can I safely use ChatGPT with customer data?

The short answer is: Not by default. When you paste raw client spreadsheets, phone numbers, or corporate emails into ChatGPT (or Claude), that data is transmitted to third-party servers. Unless you are on a strict Enterprise plan, this info can be used to train future models, leading to potential security incidents and GDPR violations.

The Solution: Local Anonymization

Instead of banning AI tools in your agency or company, the best practice is to deploy a "local gateway" approach. By replacing sensitive PII (Personally Identifiable Information) with indexed tokens like [NAME_1] or [EMAIL_1] before pasting, you can utilize 100% of the AI's analytical power without exposing a single byte of actual customer data.

How to safely process HR & payroll data with LLMs

HR professionals frequently use AI to write review summaries, analyze compensation metrics, or draft termination letters. However, sharing salaries and full names is a direct violation of internal compliance policies.

Step-by-Step AI-Safe HR Workflow:

  • Copy your internal payroll spreadsheet or text log.
  • Pass it through TableMask to turn actual financial metrics and names into secure custom tags (e.g., [NAME_1] earns [CUSTOM_1]).
  • Ask ChatGPT: "Draft a compensation review based on these parameters..."
  • Take the AI response, paste it into the De-anonymize tab, and restore real employee names in 1 millisecond.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files or text sent to any servers?

Never. TableMask is built on a 100% local-first architecture. All parsing, regular expressions, and masking happen directly inside your browser using Web Workers. You can literally disconnect your internet and the tool will work perfectly.

How does the Reverse Mapping feature work?

When you mask data, TableMask temporarily saves a secure token map (e.g., [NAME_1] = Sarah Chen) strictly inside your browser's temporary memory (RAM). When you paste the AI response back into the de-anonymize tab, it swaps tokens back to real values. Closing the browser tab wipes this map forever.

Is this compliant with GDPR and HIPAA?

Yes. Since zero data is transmitted, processed, or stored on our infrastructure, using TableMask effectively stops data exposure before it even reaches OpenAI or Anthropic servers, keeping your workflows compliant.
